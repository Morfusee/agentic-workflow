# Mapúa Malayan Digital College Website

> Official website of Mapúa Malayan Digital College (MMDC), a digital-first college under Mapúa Malayan Colleges Laguna offering flexible online college programs, certification programs, admissions guidance, scholarships, and student-focused resources for learners in the Philippines.

Mapúa Malayan Digital College is designed for students, working learners, career shifters, and professionals who want flexible, technology-driven education. The website provides official information about MMDC's academic programs, admissions process, scholarships, certifications, tuition planning tools, news, events, and student support resources.

Use this file to understand the most important public content areas of the MMDC website. For official and updated details, always refer to the linked pages.

## Primary Website

- [MMDC Homepage](https://www.mmdc.mcl.edu.ph/): Main entry point for Mapúa Malayan Digital College, including program highlights, admissions prompts, student pathways, and featured website content.
- [About MMDC](https://www.mmdc.mcl.edu.ph/about/): Overview of MMDC, its digital-first learning model, institutional background, and mission for Filipino learners.
- [Find the Learning Pathway for You](https://www.mmdc.mcl.edu.ph/find-the-learning-pathway-for-you/): Guided pathway content for students exploring which MMDC program or learning option may fit their goals.

## Academic Programs

- [BS Business Administration](https://www.mmdc.mcl.edu.ph/college-programs/business-administration/): Bachelor's degree pathway for learners pursuing business, management, operations, marketing, HR, and AI-enabled business roles.
- [BS Information Technology](https://www.mmdc.mcl.edu.ph/college-programs/information-technology/): Bachelor's degree pathway for learners pursuing technology careers such as software development, data analytics, AI-powered product development, and network or cybersecurity roles.
- [Certification Programs](https://www.mmdc.mcl.edu.ph/certifications-lead-form/): Short, flexible certification programs for learners who want job-ready skills, professional upskilling, or career development without committing immediately to a full degree.

## Admissions and Enrollment

- [Tuition Fee Calculator](https://www.mmdc.mcl.edu.ph/admissions/): Tuition planning tool that helps applicants estimate fees based on units, payment duration, and applicable scholarship discounts.
- [Apply to MMDC](https://www.mmdc.mcl.edu.ph/admissions/): Starting point for students who want to submit an application and begin the MMDC enrollment process.

## Scholarships and Financial Assistance

- [Asenso Scholarship](https://www.mmdc.mcl.edu.ph/admissions/asenso-scholarship/): Scholarship option for eligible MMDC applicants.
- [Abanse Negrense Scholarship](https://www.mmdc.mcl.edu.ph/admissions/abanse-negrense-scholarship/): Scholarship program for eligible students from Negros Occidental, excluding Bacolod City.
- [City Scholarships](https://www.mmdc.mcl.edu.ph/admissions/city-scholarships/): Scholarship opportunities connected to participating city-based programs.
- [Continuing Education Scholarship](https://www.mmdc.mcl.edu.ph/admissions/continuing-education/): Scholarship path for eligible continuing education applicants.
- [Gadget Scholarship](https://www.mmdc.mcl.edu.ph/admissions/gadget-scholarship/): Support program for eligible learners who need assistance with learning equipment or access.
- [Study Now, Pay Later Loan Program](https://www.mmdc.mcl.edu.ph/admissions/study-now-pay-later-loan-program/): Financing option for students who need flexible payment support.
- [Bukas Tuition Installment Plan](https://www.mmdc.mcl.edu.ph/admissions/bukas-tuition-installment-plan/): Tuition installment option for eligible MMDC students.
