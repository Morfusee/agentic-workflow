NGN-752: Internal MID Architecture Change — QA Blocked

What Changed

  • Each merchant account now carries the partner Internal MID publicId (not a fabricated MA-... value)
  • BIN ISO selection provisions one partner InternalMid per selected BIN ISO
  • externalMid is now a distinct display value, separate from internalMid
  • Finance-facing endpoint returns the partner publicId consistently

The Blocker

Ruby and Plat BIN ISOs showed no processor options during approval despite having Elavon configured. Only Gold BIN ISO worked.

Josh confirmed this should be tracked as a separate bug ticket.

NGN-752 is the meat of the architectural work this week. The developer made each merchant account carry the partner-owned Internal MID publicId instead of a locally fabricated MA value, separated externalMid as a distinct display value, and provisioned one InternalMid per selected BIN ISO. QA started testing the full approval pipeline on staging. Gold BIN ISO worked, but Ruby and Plat showed no processor options during approval despite having Elavon configured. QA returned the ticket as BLOCKED and the developer confirmed the processor-options issue should be a separate bug.
